Frequently Asked Questions

What size dumpster should I rent?

The size depends on the volume and type of debris. For small home projects choose 10-15 yards; major renovations may require 30-40 yards.

How long can I keep a dumpster rental?

Rental periods vary by location but typically span 7-10 days. Extensions are often available for an additional fee.

Are there items I cannot put in a dumpster?

Yes. Hazardous materials like gasoline, oil, asbestos, batteries and tires are usually prohibited.

How does delivery and pickup work?

You schedule delivery, clear the area, load your debris, then request pickup when done. Many companies allow you to be off-site during delivery and pickup.

What happens if I exceed the weight limit or fill line?

You may incur overage fees or the rental pickup may be refused until the load is corrected.

How do I choose a reliable dumpster rental company?

Look for a company with transparent pricing, clear size options, good reviews and national or multi-state reach if needed.
